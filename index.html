About Me

I am a PhD student working with the GRACE team at INRIA Paris-Saclay and the LIX at École polytechnique. I am supervised by Alain Couvreur and Martino Borello.
My research interests include Algebraic Coding Theory (both in Hamming and rank metric), Finite Geometry and applications to Cryptography.

In March 2024, I completed my Master's of Science in Mathematics at the University of Trento. There, I attended a highly selective Master's program focused on mathematical topics with direct applications in Cryptography and Coding Theory, alongside courses on computer science and information theory.
